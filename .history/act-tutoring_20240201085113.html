Paper-and-pencil (some computer-based tests are available on school days or internationally)

New Digital SAT

Format
Computer-based using an app from the College Board
Static vs. Adaptive Testing Structure
The test is adaptive, meaning the difficulty of questions in the second modules depend on your performance in the first ones
Sections/Questions
Two Sections (Each With Two Modules)

Reading and Writing
Modules 1 & 2: 27 questions and 32 minutes each (total of 54 questions and 64 minutes)

Math Modules 1 & 2: 22 questions and 35 minutes each (total of 44 questions and 60 minutes)
Essay Section
None
Total Time
2 hours and 14 minutes
Types of Questions
All multiple-choice, besides 11 student-produced math responses
Scoring
Total score: 400-1600
Score Report Release Dates
Available days after test day

At school or a testing center

Math: 58 questions, 80 minutes (broken into calculator and no-calculator sections)

FAQ's

Looking for answers about our ACT tutoring programs? Then check out these FAQs!

Who will be my ACT tutor?

Our 99th percentile tutors are ACT experts ready to teach you proven problem-solving strategies and test-taking skills to ace the test.


Do you offer scholarships or discounts for your services?

Our mission is to help every candidate realize their true potential. When you graduate from college, we want you to look back and be proud of yourself for achieving your dreams. As such, we want to make sure that your financial circumstances are not a barrier to success.

In keeping with our mission, we provide a small number of partial and full scholarships to our applicants. These are awarded after you start working with us and are based on both professional potential and financial need. Please note, these scholarships are only available for our End-to-End Admissions Consulting Package.

That said, we also want to make sure you are working with the best team, and attracting top talent is expensive. Hence, we’re not able to provide scholarships to everyone. Your project manager can tell you more about scholarship opportunities after we start the engagement.

What’s covered in my ACT tutoring program?

All our ACT tutoring programs are tailored to your individual needs. As such, what’s covered in each plan depends on the student – some students may need more help with general test-taking strategies, whereas others need more help with particular concepts.

How do you partner me with an ACT tutor?

We factor in your needs, goals, and preferences before deciding on your best-fit tutor. Selecting the right tutor is an integral part of our process – the matches we make help maximize your success!

Which ACT tutoring program is right for me?

Students need varying levels of support as they prepare for the ACT. Our tutoring programs reflect various support levels and hours. Unsure which program is best for you? Our expert team is here to walk you through your options – schedule a call with us today to learn more!
