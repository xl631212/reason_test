Meet the Panelists

Maisha
Admissions Counselor - Columbia University
Maisha has a Bachelor’s degree from Columbia University and a PhD from the Albert Einstein College of Medicine. With over a decade of experience as an admissions coach, Maisha has helped applicants gain acceptance into the country’s top colleges. Maisha is passionate about guiding students through the college application process. She loves when dreams come to fruition; she feels lucky to have been accepted into her dream college and wishes to help others feel the same way and achieve their goals. Maisha looks forward to passing along the skills and knowledge she has gained throughout her career to contribute to the growth and development of future successful academic leaders in their pursuit of higher education. As a coach, Maisha understands the challenges of applying to college. She enjoys helping students throughout the process, from choosing the best schools and programs to writing and editing personal statements. Her greatest strengths include helping candidates create timelines and breaking down the application process into manageable steps. Additionally, she excels at helping candidates determine their interests, brainstorm essay topics, and revise their essays.
